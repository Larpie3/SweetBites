FAQ & Policies

How do I place a custom order?

Use the contact form below. Provide desired date, size, flavor, and any reference images.

What is your lead time for custom cakes?

We recommend at least 3–7 days prior to the event for custom cakes depending on complexity.

Delivery & pickup

Pickup is available at our shop. Delivery can be arranged for an additional fee — provide the address in the contact form.

Payment

We accept cash on pickup and bank transfer for orders. Details will be provided after confirmation.
